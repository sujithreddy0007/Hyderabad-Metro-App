About Hyderabad Metro

Revolutionizing urban transportation in Hyderabad since 2017

Our Story

Hyderabad Metro Rail is a rapid transit system serving the city of Hyderabad and its suburbs. It is one of India's largest metro networks in terms of length and ridership.

Since 2017, Hyderabad Metro has provided safe, efficient, and comfortable transportation, transforming the way residents commute across the city.

Our Mission

Hyderabad Metro aims to provide safe, efficient, and sustainable urban transportation for the citizens of Hyderabad.

Our mission is to reduce traffic congestion, promote eco-friendly travel, and enhance connectivity across the city while providing world-class metro services.

Our Journey Through the Years

The evolution of Hyderabad Metro from inception to becoming a major urban transit system

1
2010

Project Initiation

Hyderabad Metro project was approved by the Government of India and Telangana state to improve urban transport in Hyderabad.

2
2012

Construction Begins

Construction work commenced with modern elevated and underground metro lines planned across the city.

3
2017

First Line Inauguration

The Red Line from Miyapur to LB Nagar was inaugurated, marking the start of metro services in Hyderabad.

4
2019

Network Expansion

Green Line and other corridors opened, increasing the metro network coverage in the city.

5
2020-Present

Further Growth

Extensions and new lines are under development to connect more areas of Hyderabad for better urban mobility.
